Popular Artwork Gallery

  • The Starry Night

    Painted by Vincent van Gogh in 1889, this is one of the most famous paintings in the world. It depicts the view from his room at the asylum of Saint-Paul-de-Mausole in southern France, where he was recovering from mental illness.

  • Mona Lisa

    Painted by Leonardo da Vinci in the early 16th century, this portrait of a woman has become one of the most iconic images in the world. It is on display at the Louvre Museum in Paris.

  • The Persistence of Memory

    Painted by Salvador Dali in 1931, this surrealist painting features melting clocks and a barren landscape. It has become one of the most recognizable paintings of the 20th century.

  • Guernica

    Pablo Picasso's 1937 painting depicts the horrors of war and the bombing of the Spanish town of Guernica during the Spanish Civil War. It is on display at the Museo Reina Sofía in Madrid.

  • The Kiss

    Gustav Klimt's 1908 painting depicts a couple embracing in a field of flowers. It is considered one of the greatest works of the Art Nouveau movement.

  • The Great Wave off Kanagawa

    Created by Japanese artist Katsushika Hokusai in the early 19th century, this woodblock print is one of the most famous examples of Japanese art. It depicts a towering wave about to crash down on boats below.

  • Water Lilies

    Claude Monet's series of Water Lilies paintings, created in the early 20th century, are some of the most beloved works of Impressionism. They are on display at the Musée de l'Orangerie in Paris.
