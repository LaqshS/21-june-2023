Indian Staple Dishes Tutorial

Biryani Recipe

Step 1: Wash and soak 2 cups of basmati rice for 30 minutes.

Step 2: In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil and add whole spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves.

Step 3: Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.

Step 4: Add marinated chicken, yogurt, and biryani masala. Cook for 10 minutes.

Step 5: Layer half-cooked rice over the chicken and sprinkle saffron-infused milk. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.

Step 6: Garnish with fried onions, chopped mint, and coriander leaves. Serve hot!

Chole Bhature Recipe

Step 1: Soak 1 cup of chickpeas overnight. Pressure cook them until tender.

Step 2: In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds, ginger-garlic paste, and chopped onions. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.

Step 3: Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and chole masala. Cook for a few minutes.

Step 4: Add the cooked chickpeas and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 5: For bhature, mix all-purpose flour, yogurt, baking powder, salt, and water to form a soft dough. Let it rest for 2 hours.

Step 6: Roll out the dough into circles and deep fry until golden brown. Serve with chole!

Dosa Recipe

Step 1: Soak 1 cup of rice and 1/4 cup of urad dal for 6 hours.

Step 2: Grind the soaked rice and dal to a smooth batter. Ferment overnight.

Step 3: Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladleful of batter. Spread it in a circular motion.

Step 4: Drizzle oil and cook until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy.

Step 5: Serve hot with sambar and chutney.

Butter Chicken Recipe

Step 1: Marinate boneless chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Keep it aside for 30 minutes.

Step 2: In a pan, heat butter and add chopped onions. Cook until golden brown.

Step 3: Add tomato puree, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.

Step 4: Add the marinated chicken and cook until it's tender and well-coated with the sauce.

Step 5: Finish with a dollop of butter and cream. Serve with naan or rice.

Aloo Paratha Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the dough by mixing whole wheat flour, water, and salt. Let it rest for 15 minutes.

Step 2: Boil and mash potatoes. Add chopped onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, and spices.

Step 3: Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into circles.

Step 4: Place a portion of the potato filling in the center of each circle. Fold and seal the edges.

Step 5: Roll out the stuffed dough into flatbread and cook on a hot griddle until both sides are golden brown.

Step 6: Serve hot with yogurt, pickle, or butter.

Paneer Tikka Recipe

Step 1: Cut paneer and vegetables (bell peppers, onions) into cubes.

Step 2: In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, and lemon juice.

Step 3: Add paneer and vegetables to the marinade. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.

Step 4: Thread the marinated paneer and vegetables onto skewers.

Step 5: Grill the skewers until the paneer and vegetables are cooked and slightly charred.

Step 6: Serve hot with mint chutney and salad.

Samosa Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the dough by mixing all-purpose flour, salt, and oil. Let it rest for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.

Step 3: Add boiled and mashed potatoes, peas, and spices. Cook until the filling is well combined.

Step 4: Roll out small portions of the dough and fill them with the potato mixture.

Step 5: Fold and seal the edges to form a triangular shape.

Step 6: Deep fry the samosas until they turn golden brown. Serve with chutney.

Rajma Chawal Recipe

Step 1: Soak rajma (kidney beans) overnight. Pressure cook them until tender.

Step 2: In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds, ginger-garlic paste, and chopped onions. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.

Step 3: Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook for a few minutes.

Step 4: Add the cooked rajma and simmer for 15-20 minutes.

Step 5: Serve hot with steamed rice and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
