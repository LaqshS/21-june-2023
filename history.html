Indian Historical Battles

  • Battle of Panipat - 1526 CE - A decisive victory for Babur, the founder of the Mughal Empire, over the Sultan of Delhi, Ibrahim Lodi.

  • Battle of Plassey - 1757 CE - A victory for the British East India Company over the Nawab of Bengal, which marked the beginning of British rule in India.

  • Battle of Buxar - 1764 CE - A victory for the British East India Company over the combined forces of the Nawab of Bengal, the Nawab of Awadh, and the Mughal Emperor, which solidified British control over India.

  • Battle of Seringapatam - 1799 CE - A victory for the British East India Company over the Kingdom of Mysore, which ended the Fourth Anglo-Mysore War and resulted in the death of the Mysorean ruler, Tipu Sultan.

  • Indian Rebellion of 1857 - 1857 CE - A rebellion against British rule in India, which was brutally suppressed by the British and marked the end of the Mughal Empire.
